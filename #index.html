Renana Gershoni Poranne is a Senior Scientist in the group of Prof. Dr. Peter Chen at the Laboratorium für Organische Chemie at the ETH Zürich. She was appointed in July 2017, following a two-year post-doctoral period in the same group. She completed her PhD studies under the supervision of Prof. Amnon Stanger in the Schulich Faculty of Chemistry at the Technion, working on elucidation of the properties of aromatic compounds and developing methodologies for the identification and quantification of aromaticity in polycylic aromatic hydrocarbons. Prior to that, she received her MSc Summa cum Laude for her work on functionlization of corannulene in the group of Prof. Ehud Keinan.

Dr. Gershoni Poranne's research interests lie in the field of computational physical organic chemistry, with particular emphasis on development of methods and tools for better understanding of mechanisms and physical properties of organic and organo-metallic compounds. Her work focuses on questions related to aromaticity, dispersion, metallophilic interactions, catalysis, and mechanism elucidation.
