About Me

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Interactive Geometry Lab at ETH Zurich, hosted by Prof. Olga Sorkine-Hornung. Before that, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Weizmann Institute of Science, in the group of prof. Yaron Lipman. I earned my BSc in mathematics and physics, and my PhD in computer science from the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, under supervision of Prof. Craig Gotsman. My research interestes are mainly related to geometry processing, with a strong focus on interactive performances.
